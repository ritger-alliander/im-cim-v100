    Class: GovCT1

    _General model for any prime mover with a PID governor, used primarily for combustion turbine and combined cycle units. _

    _This model can be used to represent a variety of prime movers controlled by PID governors. It is suitable, for example, for the representation of: _

    _

      _

    _

  1. gas turbine and single shaft combined cycle turbines
    2. _

    _ _

    _

      _

    _

  2. diesel engines with modern electronic or digital governors
    3. _

    _ _

    _

      _

    _

  3. steam turbines where steam is supplied from a large boiler drum or a large header whose pressure is substantially constant over the period under study
    4. _

    _

  4. simple hydro turbines in dam configurations where the water column length is short and water inertia effects are minimal.
    5. _

    _ _

    Additional information on this model is available in the 2012 IEEE report, Dynamic Models for Turbine-Governors in Power System Studies, 3.1.2.3 pages 3-4 (GGOV1).
