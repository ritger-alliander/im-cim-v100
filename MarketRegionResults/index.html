    Class: MarketRegionResults

    _Provides all Region Ancillary Service results for the DA and RT markets. The specific data is commodity type (Regulation Up, Regulation Down, Spinning Reserve, Non-spinning Reserve, or Total Up reserves) based for the cleared MW, cleared price, and total capacity required for the region. _
