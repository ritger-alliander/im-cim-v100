    Class: TimeSchedule

    _Description of anything that changes through time. Time schedule is used to perform a single-valued function of time. Use inherited 'type' attribute to give additional information on this schedule, such as: periodic (hourly, daily, weekly, monthly, etc.), day of the month, by date, calendar (specific times and dates). _
